Mission Briefing

Primary Objectives:

  • Destroy all 9 Iranian Nuclear Facilities:
    • Natanz Facility
    • Fordow Complex
    • Arak (IR-40) Reactor
    • Esfahan (Isfahan) Facility
    • Bushehr Nuclear Power Plant
    • Tehran Research Reactor
    • Saghand Uranium Mine
    • Gchine Uranium Mine
    • Ardakan Yellowcake Plant

Controls:

  • Mouse: Aim and fire missiles
  • Click & Drag: Set power and trajectory
  • Space: Deploy aircraft
  • 1-4: Select weapon type

Strategy Tips:

  • Watch for enemy interceptor missiles (red trails)
  • Defend against Iranian counterattacks (green trails)
  • Protect your base - if it's destroyed, mission fails
  • Use aircraft for precise strikes
  • Aim for direct hits for maximum damage
  • Complete mission quickly for time bonus
  • The enemy gets more aggressive as time passes
